Creators retain full ownership of their content at all times. Submitting content to BaloTV does not transfer ownership of the content to BaloTV.

This license remains active until:

If ownership of submitted content changes due to:

BaloTV reserves the right to:

  • Temporarily remove the content

If BaloTV generates revenue directly from creator content, the creator is entitled to 50% of the net revenue generated from that content unless a separate written agreement exists.

This applies when:

  • BaloTV did not financially contribute to production
  • BaloTV did not co-own the content
  • The creator independently produced the content

Revenue may include:

  • Advertising income
  • Sponsorship revenue
  • Subscription revenue
  • Content licensing revenue

5. Creator Responsibilities

By submitting content, creators confirm that:

  • They own the content or have legal permission to distribute it

6. Content Moderation

BaloTV reserves the right to review, reject, demonetize or remove content that:

  • Violates copyright laws

BaloTV may remove disputed content during investigations.

8. Platform Promotion

Creators allow BaloTV to use thumbnails, titles, clips and promotional material from submitted content for marketing and platform promotion purposes.
